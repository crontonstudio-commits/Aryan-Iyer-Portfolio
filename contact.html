Quick Answers

FAQ

How long does a project take?

It depends on scope. A short animation loop may take 1–2 weeks; a full explainer video or short film can take 4–8 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline after our initial chat.

What's your pricing like?

Pricing is project-based and depends on complexity, length, and timeline. Reach out with project details and I'll send a custom quote within 48 hours.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. I collaborate with clients across the globe. Communication is remote-friendly — video calls, email, and async feedback all work great.

What files do you deliver?

Typically MP4/MOV for animation, PNG/PSD/AI for illustrations, and PDF/JPG for storyboards. I can accommodate special format requests too.
